About The Project
The Digital CRC for California is a new UC Berkeley project in collaboration with Lt. Governor Gavin Newsom that integrates our CAFE technology into a mobile phone platform that allows Californians to grade (from A to F) how well they feel California's elected officials are working to address 6 key subjects. The interface is designed to track changes over time and to encourage to join online dialogues about key issues.
